Our Specialized Curricula

BrightPath provides expert tutoring for CAPS, DCAPS, IEB, and Cambridge learners. Explore how our training can help your child thrive academically and personally.

Curriculum Expertise

CAPS (Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement)

CAPS is South Africa's national curriculum, providing a structured and comprehensive learning path for Grades 1â€“12. BrightPath tutoring aligns fully with CAPS, offering personalized support, exam preparation, and step-by-step guidance to ensure each learner excels academically.

DCAPS (Differentiated CAPS)

DCAPS adapts CAPS for learners with moderate to severe intellectual disabilities, focusing on functional skills and life readiness. BrightPath tutors provide adaptive teaching methods, functional skill development, and holistic support to empower each learner.

IEB (Independent Examinations Board)

The IEB curriculum is flexible and internationally recognized, emphasizing critical thinking and independent learning. BrightPath tutoring prepares learners for IEB assessments, enhances problem-solving skills, and helps students achieve their highest academic potential.

Cambridge International

Cambridge offers a globally recognized education system, preparing students for university worldwide. BrightPath tutoring aligns with Cambridge standards, enhancing research, analysis, and independent study skills while opening doors to international opportunities.
